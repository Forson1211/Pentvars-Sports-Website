PU SRC Sports Academy History

The PU SRC Sports Academy was established in 2010 with the aim of promoting sports and physical education among students of Pentecost University. Over the years, the academy has grown to become one of the top sports academies in Ghana, producing talented athletes who have gone on to represent the country in various international competitions.
