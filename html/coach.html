Meet Coach Sweet Poison

Coach Sweet Poison is a highly experienced and accomplished coach with a passion for helping athletes reach their full potential. With over 10 years of coaching experience, he has worked with athletes of all levels, from beginners to professionals.

Coaching Experience:

10+ years of coaching experience.Worked with athletes of all levels, from beginners to professionals. Coached teams to multiple championships and tournament wins

Level of Coaching:

Coach Sweet Poison holds a Level 3 coaching certification, the highest level of certification in his sport. He is also a certified strength and conditioning specialist, allowing him to provide his athletes with a comprehensive training program.

Vision and Mission:

Coach Sweet Poison's vision is to create a positive and supportive training environment that allows athletes to reach their full potential. His mission is to help athletes develop the skills, confidence, and mental toughness needed to succeed at the highest levels of competition.

Methods of Coaching:

Coach Sweet Poison uses a variety of coaching methods, including:
Positive reinforcement and encouragement.
Technical skill development and correction
Conditioning and strength training
Mental preparation and visualization techniques

Methodology:

Coach Sweet Poison's coaching methodology is centered around the athlete. He believes in creating a personalized training program that addresses the unique needs and goals of each athlete. He also emphasizes the importance of hard work, dedication, and teamwork in achieving success.
