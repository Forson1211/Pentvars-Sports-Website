ABOUT US

The PU SRC Sports Academy was established in 2010 with the aim of promoting sports and physical education among students of Pentecost University. Over the years, the academy has grown to become one of the top sports academies in Ghana, producing talented athletes who have gone on to represent the country in various international competitions.

We are PU SRC Sport, a school which teaches its students to believe in themselves and their dreams and do whatever it takes to bring it into being. This will help them to bring out their inner brilliance and help them to hone their skills. This can be seen clearly and accessed from the performance of sportsmen that have been trained at our school.

Studying with us at PU SRC Sport increases your chances of becoming a pro and playing with top teams and across the world by two hundred percent. Our experienced staff will help you become the best you can be by imparting you the knowledge that have been gathered over several years in each of the sports they teach.

Make sure you study with us at PU SRC Sport for your bright future. Join us now!

Our Mission & Vission

MISSION

PU SRC Sports Academy, our mission is to empower students to achieve excellence in sports and physical education, fostering a culture of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. We strive to provide a supportive and inclusive environment that enables our students to reach their full potential and become leaders in their chosen sports.

VISSION

Our vision is to be the premier sports academy in Ghana, renowned for producing talented athletes who excel in their sports and make a positive impact in their communities. We aim to create a legacy of excellence, innovation, and integrity, inspiring future generations of students to pursue their passions and achieve greatness.
